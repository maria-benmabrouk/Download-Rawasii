ABOUT RAWASII

Rawassi is a digital platform dedicated to showcasing and preserving Algerian architectural heritage through a modern and interactive experience that combines learning and exploration. The app aims to introduce users to the beauty and diversity of Algerian architecture across different regions and eras, from historic landmarks to authentic traditional designs.

Rawassi gives users the opportunity to explore historical buildings, discover unique architectural styles, and learn the cultural stories that reflect Algeria’s rich identity. It also seeks to connect younger generations with their national heritage in a modern and accessible way.

Rawassi is not just an app, but a digital window that opens the doors of the past and highlights its beauty for present and future generations.
